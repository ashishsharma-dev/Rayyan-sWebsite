Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Panipati

Biography

Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati Akbarabadi Rehmatullah-Alaihe- jo ki Hazarat Makdoom Alauddin Ali Ahmed Sabir ke khandaan me se the.

Kaliyari Rehamtullahi silsile ke buzurg hai, aur ye Mughal bhadshah Shahjahan ke daur ke buzurg hai. Shahjahan ki Begam Mumtaz ki inteqaal ke baad, Shahjahan badshah ne apne waaziron se ailaan krwaya ke apni begham Mumtaz ki yaadgaar me ek roza banana chahata hun.

Shahjahan badshah aise buzurg ba kamaal badshah the jinke 5 waqt ke namazo ke alawa Tahajjud ki namaz bhi kabhi qaza nhi hui. Shahjahan badshah ne khwab me Taj Mahal ka naksha dekha, aur us daur me jo bhi chitrkaar the, Shahjahan ne unko us Taj Mahal ka naksha banane ko kaha jo unhone apne sapne me dekha tha, lekin koi aisa na bana paaya.

Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ke shishya jo ki Hazarat Isa Afandi ne unse arz kiya ke huzoor Shah Jahan badhsah ne ailaan kiya hai ke Mumtaaz ke yaad me mai ek roza banana chahta hun, aur koi bhi mera khuwaab me dekha hua naksha bna nhi paya hai.

Ek din Hazarat Isa Afandi ne Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ko ye bataya to Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ne sun liya, dusre din unhone unhe bataya to Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ne sun liya teesre din bataya tha to bhi sun liya, lekin chauthe din jab Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ko Hazarat Isa Afandi ne ye bataya to apni chadar Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ke chehre par uda di, uske baad apne mureed { Hazarat Isa Afandi} se poocha ke kuch dikh rha hai?

Hazrat Isa Afandi ne apne peer {Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe} se kha ke mujhe ek roza (Emmarat) nazar aa rha hai, aur is zariye Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ne Hazrat Isa Afandi ko wo Taj Mahal ka naksha jo ki Shah Jahan ne apne sapne me dekha tha wo Hazrat Isa Afandi ko dikha diya.

Uske baad wo chaadar ko kheech liya, Hazrat ne apne ghulaam Hazrat Isa Afandi se kha ke ye naksha bna kr badshaha Shah Jahan tk pohocha do. Hazrat Isa Afandi darbaar me phonche, waziron ne mughal badshah Shah Jahan se mulaqat karwaiye, aur wo naksha Mughal badshah ko paish kiya. Mughal badshah Shah Jahan us nakshe ko dekh kr behad khush hue aur usse poocha ke ye naksha kisne banaya hai.

Hazrat Isa Afandi ne mughal badhshah se kha ke ye maine banaya hai, Mughal badshah Shah Jahan ne kha ke ye naksha tune nhi banaya hai. Shah Jahan ke kehne pr Hazrat Isa Afandi ne asli raaz khol diya ke maire peer-o-murshid Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ne apne kashmo-karamat se mujhe se naksha dihaya aur tab me ye naksha yahan lekr aaya hun.

Mughal badshah Shah Jahan foran hi ghode pr sawaar hokr, us jagah pr phoche jahan Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe the, lekin jab wo wahan phonce tb tk unka inteqaal ho chuka tha. Uske baad Taj Mahal banna shuru ho gya.

Mai Raees Ahmed chishtiya Sabaria mein hazarat sayyed Faiz Alam Sabri Alighrahi se mureed hua 1988 me aur pehli baar Raeez Ahmed “Sabri” ban gya. Peheli baar Raeez Ahmed Sabri main Maqdoom Ali Ahmed Sabri, kaliyari Rehmatullah alaihe ke urs me phohonche, to Khan Kha Faizan-E-Wahid jo ki mere peer hai unse milaqat hui, uske baad unke Ja-Nasheem, Syed Ahmed Ali Sabri se hamari pehli mulaqat hui. Syed Ahmed Ali Sabri ne Raeez Ahmed Sabri se logo ke beech majme me kha ke tumhari peerane Shahjra Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe, ka mazaar jo ki Gandhi Nagar Agra me mujood hai unki Ziyarat kiya kro.

Phir maine Syed Faiz Alam ke zair-e-nizraan me Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ke 3-4 urs karaye the, uske baad Shah Faiz Alam ke inteqaal ke baad Raees Ahmed ne apne peer-o-murshid Syed Faiz Alam ke naam se Madarsa ke sand-e- buniyad rakhi 1981 me aur uska naam rakha, “Faiz-Uloom-Sabariya”.

Uske baad se Madarsa Faiz-Ul-Uloom ki buniyad ka kaam shru hua. Uske baad Sabse pehle Hazrat Shah Yaar Muhammed Chishti Sabri Panipati, Akbarbadi Rehmatullah- Alaihe ka roza ka nirmaan shuru hua, phir Masjid ka kaam shuru krwaya uske baad Shah Faiz Alam aur phir Shah Amed Ali Aligharhi Akbarabadi ka roza banwaya.

Pehle yahan ke sajjada nasheen Janab Shah Faiz Alam the, aur unke baad Hazarat Ahmed Ali yahan ke sajjada nasheen hue, the aur ab Fareed Ahmed Chishti Sabri yahan ke Sajjada nasheen hai.

Ab jo madrsa hai wo Uttar Pradesh sarkar se recognised hai. Arbi Farsi board me. Is Madarse me Hifz-E-Quran, Arbi aur Farsi ki taleem di jaati hai, aur saath hi duniyabi taleem bhi de jaat hai. Madarse me baccho ko rehna, khana, peena free me diya jaata hai.
